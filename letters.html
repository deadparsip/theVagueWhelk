Vague whelk, obscure my mortal creeds

Poetry was invented in 1993, after an accident with a tennis shoe, 3 pints of liquidated chicken and four nuns. It has since gone from strength to strength, even once being mentioned in a newspaper underneath the heading "Poetry: So what's it all about?"

Colin

It has been nearly roughly approximately exactly 4 years since your mysterious disappearance.
I still remember that morning.. the thick carpets of untouched snow lying down on the back garden so that it slightly resembled a sugarman's cappachino nightmare; a peaceful yet completely meaningless view that greeted me when I opened the front door to let you out. Almost immediately your huge white furry cat body was lost from view as you frolicked about, in a slightly graceless, masculine gallop. Little did I know, that would be the last time I would see you.
